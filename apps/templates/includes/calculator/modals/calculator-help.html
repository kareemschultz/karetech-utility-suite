2025 Tax Updates
  • New tax threshold: $130,000 (up from $100,000)
  • Reduced tax rate: 25% flat rate
  • Child allowance: $10,000 per child monthly
  • First $50,000 of overtime is tax-free
  • First $50,000 of second job income is tax-free

Important Notes
